§ 08

Principles

Five short rules. The system breaks if any of them slip.

Rules
i
Hairlines, never strokes.
One border weight. If you reach for a heavier line, the layout needs more space, not more ink.
ii
Mono is metadata.
Dates, indices, sizes, captions, file names — anything procedural. Always uppercase, always tracked.
iii
Warmth, not white.
Paper is #f8f6f1, ink is #0a0a0a. Pure white and pure black are off limits.
iv
No accent color.
If something needs emphasis, increase its size or give it more space.
v
Stillness with one moving thing.
The cube tumbles. Everything else holds. When the cube comes apart, it stays apart only long enough to make way for content.
